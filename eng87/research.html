Mechatronics and Controls Laboratory

  • Focuses research on servos and precision control for applications in medical devices, nano-technology and manufacturing.

Bionics Lab

  • Focuses on developing wearable bio-technology to aid in recovery or motor function of the body and medical robotics for surgical and medicinal applications.

Robotics & Mechanisms Laboratory

  • Build, wire, and program robots to compete in international competitions and participate in National Challenges.

Modeling of Complex Thermal Systems Laboratory

  • Use simulation software, such as MatLab, to predict and optimize the thermal flow of complex systems.
